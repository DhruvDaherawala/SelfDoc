Suggestions

You can do these things to stay safe from diabetes

1. Monitor blood sugar as advised.
2. Take prescribed meds as directed.
3. Eat a balanced diet, watch carbs.
4. Exercise regularly, consult your doctor.
5. Maintain a healthy weight.
6. Manage stress through relaxation techniques.
7. Schedule regular check-ups, eye/foot exams, BP, cholesterol.
8. Daily foot care and proper footwear.
9. Store meds correctly.
10. Stay hydrated, choose water over sugary drinks.
